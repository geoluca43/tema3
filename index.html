Our story

The idea of Anima festival first became the real experience in the year of 2017. Its creators concieved it as an affair meant to offer young souls the escape and connection they seek. Bringing people together, uniting minds, ears and eyes through the power of dance and music, the three stages placed in the wonderful setting of Bradenburg Gate in Berlin promise to awake even the numbest of hearts. Having won numerous awards over the years, our festival is considered to be more than an experience, more than a feeling. It is all about music, people and sensations.

Join us and together we will live the greatest of journeys, writing a magical story.

See you in Berlin, in 2022.

Our music

Stages

Touch

Experience the power of touch with the help of the most well known bands in the field of electric music: Purely Red, Les Hearts, Light Division.

Feel

Allow feelings to rise and moments to bloom while listening to the wonderful tracks of James Joyce, The Deaf Lion and Octavius the First.

Hear

The stage that is all about the fantasy sound, connection between the senses and minds. The artists playing: Silviq, Red Masters and Allan DeBoife.

See

Visual projections, sounds and live animations. Let the two senses fusion and live a unique experience with the help of: Seven P's and Charming Skin.
